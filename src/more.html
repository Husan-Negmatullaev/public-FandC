Sobha Hartland
Crest Grande

The Crest Grande residential building is located in the Sobha Hartland neighbourhood, which has a total area of 8 million sqft. The complex has good transport accessibility due to its location near Ras Al Khor Road and Al Meydan Road. Residents of Crest Grande will be able to walk and relax amongst numerous green spaces within the territory of the Sobha Hartland community.

The open green spaces in the entire development occupy 2.4 million sqft of the neighbourhood’s territory, with 3 luscious green parks called Greens West Park, Central Park and Greens East Park which are all connected by a central pathway. The community offers residents and guests all the necessary infrastructure to live a hassle-free life, such as North London Collegiate School and Hartland International School, as well as Hartland Mall with elite international brands, many dining options and much more. The strategic location near the center of Dubai provides fast access to some of the most popular attractions of the emirate, such as the Dubai Fountains, The Dubai Mall, the Burj Khalifa and Dubai Opera.

The average rental price for a 1-bedroom apartment in Sobha Hartland constitutes AED 80,000 per annum. Therefore the estimated ROI of real estate in Crest Grande will be 6%. By purchasing property in the Crest Grande building, investors will be eligible to apply for a 3-year investor visa, the benefits of which include the right to reside in the UAE, family sponsorship, opening a bank account, acquiring a UAE driving license and much more.
