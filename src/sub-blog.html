DAMAC Properties Will Have An Open House Event Dedicated to Safa Two

16 th June 2022
Все статьи →

One of the leading UAE developers, DAMAC Properties, is set to host an exclusive Open House on the 18th June in Carson B, DAMAC Hills. The long awaited event will take place between 10AM and 8PM, where potential real estate investors will have the opportunity to learn more about the recently launched Safa Two development, in association with the luxury jewellery de GRISOGONO. The stunning project follows in the footsteps of the highly successful Safa One development in the verdant area of Al Safa, offering a stunning view of the iconic Dubai Canal and the Burj Khalifa.

The ultra-refined tower of 80+ levels will have a distinctive scarlet theme, as it will pay tribute to a signature gem from founder Fawaz Gruosi’s collection, which is a highly sought-after 45.39 carat natural ruby ring ‘Virgin Scarlet’. That being said, Safa Two will be illuminated in red and house expansive leafy spaces across the residential units and communal spaces. The 2 towers will be interconnected at the base, middle and crown by exquisite features, including a ruby heart at the center on the 60th floor, with a beautiful floating pool.

Among the defining features of Safa Two, it is definitely worth mentioning the 'Fog Forest', which will be complemented by lush landscape and a manmade fog, as well as laser icon shows, F&B outlets, and a gorgeous observation deck. For an adrenaline rush, a Glass Slide will be built on the top level of the upcoming tower, which will lead from the Fog Forest to the Edge Walk. Not forgetting an artificial beach pool, a public elevated glass pool and a media screen, among other recreational offerings that can all be found within Safa Two.
